36 Years Of Undefeated Success

Since 1986, eclGlobal has built a strong foundation in design education, combining creativity with career-focused training. Our journey began with a vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and real-world industry demands—and we’ve been fulfilling that vision ever since.

For over 36 years, we’ve remained committed to quality, innovation, and hands-on learning. Our undefeated success lies in consistently producing industry-ready professionals through expert mentorship, practical exposure, and a curriculum that evolves with the times.

This legacy is not just a milestone—it’s a movement. Thousands of students have transformed their passion into thriving careers with us. At eclGlobal, we don’t just teach design—we shape the future of the interior and fashion industries, one creative mind at a time.
